Whose stories are being told? We hear a lot of news about some countries and not a lot of news about others. Why is that? Is it because there are less people doing less news worthy things? Is a lack of news buerus?

This map attempts to hightlight whose voices are getting heard. Each country is colored showing how many podcasts are being made about each country as a ratio to the population that the country.

Darker countries mean more coverage per individual. Lighter countries might mean people's stories aren't being told.

Click on a country to see the list of the latest podcast in the region.

NPR podcasts were collected going back to 2012. All podcasts that meantioned the countries's name and where in the category of "News" or "World" were selected.

Population data comes from the world bank's population API for 2014.
